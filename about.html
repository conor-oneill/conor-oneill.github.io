The Purpose Of This Site

I'm starting this off as a way to display work that I do and share my thoughts and findings. I'm aiming to explore a dataset a week in 2016, demonstrating application of technique and interesting findings with either practical applications or just satisfying curiosity. Read more here.

Why Datum? Dunno really. It's the singular of 'data' and I felt like the site needed a name. Might change it later. The rest of this site is also likely to change significantly. If you find any bugs or something doesn't work well on your device or browser, please let me know (email below)!

About Me

My name is Conor O'Neill and I live in Brisbane, Australia.

I like science

I studied physics at the University of Queensland (B.Sc. (Hons) 2014) and UC Berkeley (exchange) and have been engaged in astrophysics research since 2011 at UQ (Australia), the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (California, USA), Gemini South Observatory (Chile), and Gemini North Observatory (Hawai'i, USA).

I'm interested in all sciences, but outside of physics get most excited about areas that blend multiple fields, such as biophysics, nueroscience, nanotech, and AI.

I like technology

We live in The Future™ and I love what technology has enabled us to do. I'm most excited about where artificial intelligence has been taking us and what the future will look like from this aspect.

I like travelling

I enjoy travelling immensly and have been fortunate enough to do it quite a bit in my life. I've now been to every continent except Antarctica (it's on the to-do list), and would like to travel more widely on those continents. Until recently, Switzerland was my favourite place by a good margin. Now Hawai'i contests for that title, and of course I have a soft spot and ever-growing love for Australia.
